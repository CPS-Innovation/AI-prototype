Case Background

Factual Summary
Two suspects, Mortimer Blorgstein and Thessaly Vexley, robbed Quibblesworth Fine Jewellers in Little Puddington on 19 August 2024. Blorgstein assaulted the proprietor with a crowbar, causing serious injuries, while Vexley acted as a lookout and threatened an employee. They stole approximately £127,000 worth of jewellery and fled in a dark blue saloon car. Witnesses provided a partial registration number and evidence suggesting the robbery was premeditated. Both victims received medical care, with the proprietor hospitalized for three days.
